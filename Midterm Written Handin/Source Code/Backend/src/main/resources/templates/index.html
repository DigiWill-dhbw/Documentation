DigiWill

Setup your digital Will

DigiWill is going to be a digital will-keeper which helps you maintain your will and execute it in the event of your death.

DigiWill will give you the ability to specify actions the tool should take in the event of your death. These actions could range from sending pre-written emails to specified recipients to deleting messages and/or accounts from social media sites.
